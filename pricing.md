## Parcel Journey Pricing

Parcel Journey (tracking + notification) is priced per **delivered order only** — you don't pay for RTS parcels. Same rate for Chat or SMS.

| Plan | Rate | Delivered orders (at 70% delivery) | Cost |
|---|---|---|---|
| Free Trial | Free (Chat only) | — | ₱0 |
| Starter | ₱1 per 5 delivered | 2,100 delivered | ₱420/mo |
| Growth | ₱1 per 5 delivered | 7,000 delivered | ₱1,400/mo |
| Scale | ₱1 per 5 delivered | 21,000 delivered | ₱4,200/mo |

## ROI Context

A seller at 30% RTS loses approximately ₱430 per failed parcel (forward shipping + return shipping + packaging + damage + unrealized profit).

| Plan | Orders | RTS parcels (at 30%) | Monthly RTS bleed | Artemis total cost | Break-even RTS reduction |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Starter | 3,000 | 900 | ₱387,000 | ₱1,919 | 0.5% |
| Growth | 10,000 | 3,000 | ₱1,290,000 | ₱5,399 | 0.4% |
| Scale | 30,000 | 9,000 | ₱3,870,000 | ₱14,199 | 0.4% |

Artemis pays for itself if the seller reduces RTS by less than 1%.

## Realistic Monthly Revenue Projections

Assumptions:
- PH COD SaaS niche, launching with Pancake POS sellers as target
- Free trial conversion rate: 15-25% (industry avg for freemium B2B SaaS)
- Customer mix: 65% Starter, 25% Growth, 10% Scale
- Parcel Journey adoption: ~50% of paying users, averaging 60% of their delivered order volume
- Delivery rate assumption: 70% across all tiers
- Churn: ~8%/mo early, improving to ~5%/mo as product matures

### Key milestones

- **Break-even (1-person operation):** ~30 paying customers (~₱73k MRR) — covers infra, SMS costs, and modest salary
- **First hire:** ~80 customers (~₱188k MRR) — enough to bring on support/CS
- **Sustainable business:** ~150 customers (~₱362k MRR) — small team, healthy margins
- **Serious SaaS:** ~300+ customers (~₱730k+ MRR) — ₱8.8M+ ARR, real business

## Notes

- Pages: unlimited on all plans (no page cap — tier is based on total monthly orders across all connected Pancake pages)
- Parcel Journey is charged on delivered orders only — RTS parcels are not billed
- Above 30,000 orders/mo: custom/enterprise pricing — contact sales
- Annual discount: TBD (consider 2 months free on annual billing)
- Free trial: 14 days, up to 500 orders, 1 month data retention, Parcel Journey via Chat only (free), no credit card required
